TLDR2Markdown Gmail Add-on

Effective Date: 07-03-2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Melih Gultekin ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use the TLDR2Markdown Gmail Add-on (the "Add-on"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

When you use the Add-on, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Email Content: To provide the summarization service, the Add-on temporarily accesses the content (body, subject, sender, recipient, labels) of emails in your Gmail inbox that are labelled as "TLDR" (or a similar label, as configured by the developer). This data is accessed and processed only to generate the AI summary.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the Add-on, such as the number of emails summarized, the frequency of use, and any errors that occur. This helps us improve the Add-on.
  • Anonymous, Aggregated Data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated data about the use of the Add-on. This data does not identify you personally and is used for statistical analysis and to improve the Add-on.
  • Error Logs: If the Add-on encounters an error, we may collect error logs that include information about the error and the state of the Add-on at the time of the error.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide the Service: To analyze emails and generate AI summaries.
  • To Improve the Add-on: To analyze usage data and error logs to improve the performance, reliability, and features of the Add-on.
  • To Provide Support: To respond to your questions and provide support.

4. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with the following categories of recipients:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us provide the Add-on, such as "Google Cloud Platform for AI processing". We ensure that these service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information. Specifically, the email content is sent to Google Gemini models for AI processing. Gemini's privacy policy can be found at https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/13594961?hl=en#privacy_notice .
  • Legal Authorities: We may disclose your information to legal authorities if required by law or legal process.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

We do *not* sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include: [Best Practices for Google Apps Script](https://developers.google.com/apps-script/guides/support/best-practices). However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Add-on and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. Specifically: "Email content is only stored temporarily in the memory during the AI summarization process and summary is exported to your Google Docs account immediately after the summary is generated. Usage data is **not** retained.

7. Your Rights

You may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to:

  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal information.
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your personal information.
  • Restriction: Request that we restrict the processing of your personal information.
  • Data Portability: Request to receive your personal information in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format and to transmit that data to another controller.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at mmelihgultekin@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

The Add-on is not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at mmelihgultekin@gmail.com.
